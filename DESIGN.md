# PitchTank Design System

## Product context

PitchTank is a private AI pitch-rehearsal room for early-stage founders. The interface should feel candid, challenging, and useful rather than futuristic or decorative.

## Direction

**The Pressure Room** combines an editorial founder notebook with a live broadcast control room. It borrows Forkd's blunt typography, human copy, strong contrast, and purposeful movement without copying its recipe motifs or orange palette.

## Tokens

- Display: Archivo Black
- Body: DM Sans
- Data and labels: IBM Plex Mono
- Ink: `#10110f`
- Cold paper: `#f6f7f0`
- Fog: `#e7e9e1`
- Muted: `#62665d`
- Signal lime: `#c8ff3d`
- Deep lime: `#83b800`

## Layout and motion

- Creative editorial layouts on marketing pages, disciplined rails and tables in the product
- Controls use 10px corners, cards use 16px, and pills are reserved for statuses
- Reveal, ticker, and interaction motion only; all motion stops under reduced-motion
